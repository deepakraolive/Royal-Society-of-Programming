Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use the Royal Society of Programming platform, we collect information you provide directly to us. This includes your email address and password for account creation, and educational data you choose to share — including assignments, learning progress, dates, and optional profile descriptions. We do not collect sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers or credit card details directly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide to operate and improve our educational courses. Your academic information is processed to generate learning summaries, progress charts, and insights about your performance in Artificial Intelligence concepts. We may use aggregated, anonymized data for analytical purposes to understand usage trends and improve the Royal Society of Programming's features.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and access controls. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

The Royal Society of Programming does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may use trusted service providers for hosting, analytics, or email delivery — these partners are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export all your academic data and certificates from your account settings. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at anzarhussainlone@gmail.com.
