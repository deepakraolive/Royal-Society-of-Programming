Lead Instructor

Er. Anzar Hussain Lone

Assistant Professor, Dept of CSE, LPU

Anzar Hussain Lone is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Lovely Professional University, Punjab. With a strong academic background, he holds a B.Tech and M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, having completed his master’s degree from National Institute of Technology (NIT) Srinagar. He is also a two-time GATE qualifier, demonstrating his deep understanding of core computer science concepts.

With over three years of teaching experience, he has been actively involved in academic instruction, research, and mentoring. His primary research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Machine Learning applications. Mr. Lone has authored four research papers in the domain of Artificial Intelligence, and is currently guiding 2 masters students and 15 B.Tech students on research and innovation projects.

An ardent researcher and educator, he has conducted several workshops and training programs on Artificial Intelligence and Prompt Engineering for students and educators. His academic and professional contributions reflect his commitment to fostering innovation, critical thinking, and applied research in emerging technologies.

Syllabus Overview

Course Structure
& Curriculum

Duration 60 Hrs
Mode Online
Level Advanced
01

Week 1: AI + Python Fundamentals

Introduction to AI paradigms, setting up robust environments, and mastering core Python programming.

  • Day 1 AI History & Setup (Anaconda, Git)
  • Day 2 Python Core: Syntax & Data Types
  • Day 3 Control Flow & Functions
  • Day 4 Object-Oriented Programming
  • Day 5 File Handling & Error Management
02

Week 2: Data Science & EDA

Data wrangling, cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and powerful visual storytelling techniques.

  • Day 1 NumPy Arrays & Math Operations
  • Day 2 Pandas DataFrame & Series
  • Day 3 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
  • Day 4 Matplotlib & Seaborn Visualization
  • Day 5 Real-world EDA Mini-Project
03

Week 3: Machine Learning Algorithms

Deep dive into supervised learning, classification techniques, and foundational regression models.

  • Day 1 Intro to Sklearn & Linear Regression
  • Day 2 Logistic Regression & Classification
  • Day 3 Decision Trees Construction
  • Day 4 Support Vector Machines (SVM)
  • Day 5 Algorithm Selection & Tradeoffs
04

Week 4: Evaluation & Ensemble

Model metrics, cross-validation strategies, Random Forests, boosting algorithms, and tuning.

  • Day 1 Evaluation Metrics (ROC, AUC, F1)
  • Day 2 Cross-validation & Overfitting
  • Day 3 Random Forests & Bagging
  • Day 4 Gradient Boosting (XGBoost, LighGBM)
  • Day 5 Hyperparameter Tuning (GridSearch)
05

Week 5: Unsupervised & Deep Learning

Advanced clustering algorithms, dimensionality reduction, and an introduction to neural networks.

  • Day 1 K-Means & Hierarchical Clustering
  • Day 2 PCA & Dimensionality Reduction
  • Day 3 Intro to Neural Networks
  • Day 4 Forward & Backpropagation
  • Day 5 Building MLPs with TensorFlow/Keras
06

Week 6: CNN, RNN & Capstone

Master deep learning for visual and sequential tasks culminating in a comprehensive real-world project.

  • Day 1 CNN Architectures (VGG, ResNet)
  • Day 2 Image Classification Project
  • Day 3 Sequence Models & RNN/LSTM
  • Day 4 NLP Foundations & Text Processing
  • Day 5 Final Capstone Presentation
4 Hands-on Projects
